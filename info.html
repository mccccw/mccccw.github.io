Welcome to MCCW

MCCW is a modded Minecraft survival server focused on Create, automation, building, and long-term world progression.

This server was built and configured from the ground up, with a custom mod setup that includes Create and MCCW, creating a unique survival experience that goes beyond standard gameplay.

Whether you enjoy building large bases, creating automated systems, designing factories, or exploring a modded world, MCCW is made for players who want a more creative and technical Minecraft experience.

Server Features

  • Modded Survival
  • Create Mod
  • Automation & Machines
  • Custom Builds
  • Technical Progression
  • Player Economy Potential
  • Long-Term Survival World

What Makes MCCW Different

MCCW is not just another random modded server. The server setup, modpack structure, and overall experience were put together manually to create a smoother and more enjoyable survival environment.

The goal is to build a world where players can:

  • create factories
  • build storage systems
  • automate farms
  • design train networks
  • expand bases and cities
  • work on large technical and decorative projects

If you enjoy Create-based survival, engineering, progression, and building something meaningful over time, MCCW is the right place.

Server Rules

General Rules

  1. Be respectful to all members - no harassment, hate speech, or bullying
  2. No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps
  3. Keep conversations appropriate - no NSFW content
  4. Follow Discord's Terms of Service at all times
  5. Use channels for their intended purpose

Minecraft Server Rules

  1. No griefing or destroying other players' builds
  2. No hacking, cheating, or using unfair mods (e.g. X-ray, aimbot)
  3. Respect land claims and property
  4. No exploiting bugs or glitches
  5. Keep builds appropriate - no offensive content
  6. No bug abuse such as TNT duping

Chat Rules

  1. No advertising without permission
  2. No excessive toxicity or drama
  3. Do not impersonate staff or other players
  4. Keep language appropriate - swearing is allowed only in moderation

Staff & Punishments

  1. Listen to staff members at all times
  2. Punishments may include warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans
  3. Attempting to bypass punishments will result in stricter action

Fair Play

  1. Play fair and don't ruin others' experience
  2. No scamming or stealing items
  3. Report rule breakers to staff instead of arguing

Agreement

By staying in this server, you agree to follow these rules.
